Nextcloud Tasks lets you manage your tasks and to-do lists directly from your Android device, synchronized with your Nextcloud server.

Features:
- View and manage all your Nextcloud task lists
- Create, edit, and complete tasks
- Organize tasks with priorities and due dates
- Automatic synchronization with your Nextcloud server
- Material Design 3 interface
- Dark mode support

Requirements:
- A Nextcloud server with the Tasks app installed
- Android 8.0 or higher
